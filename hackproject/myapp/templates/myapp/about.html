About Zeta

Zeta is a community-driven platform dedicated to reducing food waste by connecting people who have surplus food with those who need it. Our mission is to create a sustainable and compassionate world where no food goes to waste.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce food waste by connecting individuals, restaurants, and businesses with surplus food to those in need through a seamless digital platform, promoting responsible consumption and redistribution. We aim to create a sustainable ecosystem where donors can choose to earn or give, fostering a community-driven approach to tackling food insecurity while making food sharing both impactful and rewarding

Our Vision

To create a sustainable ecosystem where surplus food is efficiently redistributed, reducing food waste and hunger while empowering individuals and businesses to benefit from responsible sharing
